Inspiring Zero-Waste Initiatives!

Kamikatsu, Japan

Since 2003, Kamikatsu replaced incineration with a 45-category recycling system, achieving over 80% waste recycling.

Singapore Polytechnic

SP researchers recycle incineration ash into foam glass insulation, partnering with the Waste Management & Recycling Association.

Roubaix, France

Since 2014, Roubaix launched a Zero Waste Challenge, with 70% of families cutting waste by at least 50%, and 25% reducing it by over 80%.
